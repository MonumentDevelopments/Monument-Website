Your privacy

Privacy Policy

How we collect, use and protect any personal information you share with us.

Last updated · 14 June 2026

Monument Property Developments Ltd (“Monument”, “we”, “us” or “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, and how we look after it when you use our website or get in touch with us.

1. Who we are

Monument Property Developments Ltd is a residential property developer based in London, United Kingdom. For the purposes of UK data protection law (the UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018), we are the “data controller” of the personal data you provide to us.

If you have any questions about this policy or your data, you can contact us at hasan@monumentdevelopments.co.uk.

2. The information we collect

We only collect information you choose to give us, primarily through our contact and site-enquiry form. This may include:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your phone number (if you provide it)
  • Details about a site, land or enquiry you choose to share, such as a location, postcode or description
  • The nature of your enquiry (for example, whether you are a landowner, an introducer, or have a general question)

We do not knowingly collect any special category (sensitive) personal data, and we do not collect payment or financial details through this website.

3. How we use your information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your enquiry and correspond with you about it
  • Assess any site or land opportunity you submit
  • Arrange a finder’s fee where you have introduced a site that we go on to acquire
  • Keep a record of our communications with you

We will not use your information for unrelated purposes, and we will never sell your data to third parties.

4. Our lawful basis for using your data

Under UK data protection law, we rely on the following lawful bases:

  • Consent, when you submit our form, you are asking us to contact you about your enquiry.
  • Legitimate interests, to respond to enquiries and run our property development business, in a way that does not override your rights.

5. How your data is stored

Enquiries submitted through our website are processed and stored using Netlify, our website hosting and form provider, and are forwarded to us by email. We keep enquiry records only for as long as necessary to deal with your enquiry and for our legitimate business records, after which they are deleted.

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to keep your information secure. Our website is served over a secure (HTTPS) connection.

6. Sharing your information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share it only where necessary with trusted service providers who help us operate (such as our website host and email provider), or where we are required to do so by law.

7. Cookies

This website does not use tracking or advertising cookies. Our hosting provider may set essential cookies necessary for the site to function and to protect against spam. No personal profiling is carried out.

8. Your rights

Under UK data protection law you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Ask us to delete your data (where we have no overriding reason to keep it)
  • Object to or restrict how we use your data
  • Withdraw your consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hasan@monumentdevelopments.co.uk. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the UK’s Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised “last updated” date.

10. Contact us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at hasan@monumentdevelopments.co.uk.
