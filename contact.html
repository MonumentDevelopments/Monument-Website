02 / About

Our story

Eighteen years of building well, in London.

Monument Property Developments is a residential developer specialising in Build to Rent and Build to Sell homes — known for design-led schemes, sustainable construction and a reputation for getting the details right.

The beginning

From a single site to a trusted name.

Founded eighteen years ago, Monument has steadily grown into a respected name in London's property landscape.

With a consistent commitment to high-quality developments, we've completed 26 schemes and built a reputation for excellence. Our dedicated team focuses on creating innovative, sustainable spaces that meet the evolving needs of homeowners, tenants and investors. Over the years we've cultivated strong relationships across the industry — and our passion for exceptional craftsmanship continues to drive our growth.

Our philosophy

We don't just build properties. We build places people are proud to call home.
